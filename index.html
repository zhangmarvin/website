about me

I recently graduated with a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley. I worked with Professor Sergey Levine in the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Lab.

For the spring 2022 semester, I was a lecturer at UC Berkeley for two courses, CS 182/282A (Introduction to Deep Learning) and CS 189 (Introduction to Machine Learning).

For more information, please refer to my: CV / Google Scholar / Dissertation

I also did my undergrad at UC Berkeley, where, beyond research, I was involved in various activities involving education, music, and music education.
