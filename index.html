about me

I recently graduated with a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley. I worked with Professor Sergey Levine in the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Lab.

For the spring 2022 semester, I am serving as a lecturer for two courses, CS 182/282A (Introduction to Deep Learning) and CS 189 (Introduction to Machine Learning).

My current research interests include:

  • meta learning
  • methods for handling distribution shift

Previously, I have broadly worked on:

  • deep learning for robotics
  • model based reinforcement learning
  • deep generative modeling

I also did my undergrad at UC Berkeley, where, beyond research, I was involved in various activities involving education, music, and music education.
